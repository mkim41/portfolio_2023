SENSING FASHION

‚Äî identity and environmental design

Sensing Fashion, the 2023 RISD Museum exhibit, asks: What happens when a garment designed to be worn enters a museum as a crisp, new envelope without the potential of wear and tear? The textures of these donated second-hand clothes were observed with a microscopic camera, and the resulting investigations were projected on the gallery walls for an intimate experience with the fabric. The identity and exhibit graphics were designed to illustrate the woven patterns of the textile and to complement the immersive sight of the garments.
